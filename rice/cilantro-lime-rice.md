Cilantro Lime Rice
=================

Ingredients
-----------
- Jasmin rice: 1 cup
- chicken broth
- garlic salt
- lime
- cilantro
- green onions

Instructions:
-------------

Add Jasmin Rice
1:1.25 rice/liquid ratio for rice cooker
liquid = chicken broth
Add pepper and garlic salt

Cook in rice cooker

Add chopped cilantro, chopped green onions
Add juice from 1 lime.
serve
